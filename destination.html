Oriental Mindoro Destination

Why you visit Oriental Mindoro?

Oriental Mindoro has refreshing and amazing beaches away from the city's noise and chaos that are ideal for a relaxing vacation. Bulalacao has a lot of promising islands to explore aside from Puerto Galera, which is known for its dive sites and white sand beaches. Tourists can go island hopping to see long sandbar beaches with crystal clear waters on islands such as Suguicay, Aslom, and Target.

Resources

Mindoro is a popular tourist destination for nature lovers due to its pristine coral reefs and dramatic cliffs. Mindoro's tourist attractions include tropical sand beaches, fishing villages, and nearby impenetrable hills. Mindoro is divided into two distinct areas by an extremely rugged mountain range. The more prosperous Mindoro Oriental has nightlife and scuba diving, whereas the more remote Mindoro Occidental has spectacular mountain hikes and primitive tribal villages. A vacation in Mindoro allows you to see the endangered Mindoro dwarf buffalo and go island hopping along the turquoise blue waters.

Best Time To Visit Mindoro Island

The best time to visit this island is between April and May, which is also the time when the beaches get really crowded. Try to avoid the monsoon season i.e. between June to December as you can really enjoy the beauty of this by getting drenched frequently but the waterfalls will be a great place for a getaway during this season. To avoid the monsoon season you can also visit between January and March.

There are several ways to go to Mindoro from Manila:

  • By air: Take a flight from Manila to San Jose Airport in Occidental Mindoro, or to the Caticlan Airport in Aklan for those going to the island of Romblon).
  • By sea: Take a ferry from the Batangas Port to the Calapan Port in Oriental Mindoro. There are also ferries that go to the port of Roxas in Oriental Mindoro and to the islands of Romblon and Boracay.
  • By land and sea: Take a bus from Manila to Batangas Pier, then take a ferry to Calapan or to the other ports in Mindoro and nearby islands.
  • By private vehicle: Drive from Manila to Batangas Pier, then bring your car on the ferry to Mindoro.

Top 10 Recommended Destination

  1. Puerto Galera - famous for its stunning beaches and dive sites
  2. Tamaraw Falls - a majestic waterfall nestled in a forested area
  3. Apo Reef Natural Park - a protected area known for its diverse marine life and coral reefs
  4. White Island - a picturesque sandbar surrounded by crystal- clear waters
  5. Mt. Halcon - a challenging climb for experienced hikers Lubang Island - a remote island with pristine beaches and historical sites
  6. Muelle Bay - a scenic bay perfect for sunset watching and water sports
  7. Talipanan Falls - a hidden gem with a refreshing natural pool
  8. Sabang Beach - a quieter alternative to Puerto Galera's beaches
  9. Calapan City - the capital city of Oriental Mindoro with historical landmarks and cultural attractions.
